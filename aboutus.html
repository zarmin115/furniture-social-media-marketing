Digital & Social Media Marketing Mini Project

This website is developed as a Digital & Social Media Marketing Mini Project for academic and learning purpose. It demonstrates how a furniture brand can build its online presence using SEO, Social Media Marketing, WhatsApp Marketing, and Digital Advertisements.

The objective of this project is to showcase product categories, create professional branding, and provide a modern user-friendly furniture website layout.

About Phoenix Furniture

Phoenix Furniture is a modern office furniture brand that specializes in premium quality revolving chairs designed for comfort, productivity, and professional workspaces.

Our product range includes Boss Chairs, Office Chairs, Ergonomic Chairs, Visitor Chairs and Gaming Chairs. Each chair is designed to deliver durability, premium comfort, and modern aesthetics for corporate and home office setups.

Why Digital Marketing for Furniture?

  • Furniture products can be promoted through attractive visuals
  • Easy audience reach using Instagram and Facebook ads
  • Product discovery becomes easier with category-based pages
  • Digital marketing is cost-effective and measurable
  • Builds strong brand identity and customer engagement
